The 2026 grant opportunities in China for young foreign researchers are primarily offered through the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC). These programs are designed to attract and support talented international scientists, encouraging them to undertake research in China and foster long-term partnerships with local researchers.

üìã Overview of NSFC Grant Programs

The NSFC's grant programs for foreign scholars include several funding levels and disciplinary fields. Young scholars with a Ph.D. earned within the last six years are eligible to apply, provided they have relevant experience in basic research or post-doctoral work.
